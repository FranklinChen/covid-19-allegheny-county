Important announcements

Friday, November 19, 2021: FDA and CDC approved boosters for all adults.

Tuesday, November 2, 2021: the CDC recommends everyone ages 5 and older get a COVID-19 vaccine. See details on which vaccines are available.

Thursday, October 22, 2021: the CDC expands eligibility for COVID-19 booster shots: Pfizer-BioNTech, Moderna, Johnson & Johnson. Check the list of who is eligible.

Friday, September 24, 2021: the FDA and CDC authorize booster dose of Pfizer vaccine to certain adults who were fully vaccinated by Pfizer at least six months ago. Please check the official CDC statement for eligibility criteria.

PA order effective 12:01 AM, Tuesday, September 7, 2021: requires masking in schools, early learning and child care settings
